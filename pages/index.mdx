# Introduction
 
Welcome to Just Da Notes. This app is intended to share notes taken on interesting developer topics, from every day functions, to methodology, architecture, design and even certifications.

Feel free to browse. This app was built using Nextra.

## What is Nextra?

A **simple**, **powerful** and **flexible** site generation framework with everything you love from Next.js.
