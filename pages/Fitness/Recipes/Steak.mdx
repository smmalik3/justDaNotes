## Instructions

### Prep
1. Season steak on all sides
    - Add salt
    - Add black pepper
    - Add garlic powder 
2. Place in sous vide bag and vacuum seal

### Cooking
1. Place in sous vide water bath
2. Set sous vide to 130 degrees F for 2 hours
3. Remove from bag and use a torch to give a final sear to the steak
4. Cut into quarter inch slices and serve
