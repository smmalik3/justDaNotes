# TYFSO

## Premise

A coffee shop that sells handmade rugs. Customers don't have to buy rugs, but sit amongst all the rugs, the store front is top to bottom, wall to wall, decked out in rugs.

All types of coffee is served included Turkish coffee.

Tea is also served, including Persian tea.
